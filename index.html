Hello! We are creators of nuka. All of us have used stationery for years, so we know all benefits and problems related to it.

We saw advantages of digital notes but realized: nothing can replace the tactile experience of real materials. So, we decided to add modern technologies to stationery, and created radically new things – eternal notebook and pencil.

Fired by this idea, we left school and started focusing only on nuka. Together with our partners, we have worked for 2 years to tailor business strategy, finalize technologies, work on prototypes and run production in China. Hope you are ready to change habitual things together with us.
